# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project has adopted the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward others
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Accept responsibility and apologize to those affected by our mistakes
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Instances of behavior that violates this Code of Conduct may be reported to the project team at:

**[conduct@openjobspec.org](mailto:conduct@openjobspec.org)**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
